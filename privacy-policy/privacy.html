Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our learning platform across web, mobile, and tablet devices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following details during registration or usage:

  • Student name
  • Mobile number
  • Email address
  • Login credentials (OTP-based authentication)
  • Parent/guardian contact details (if applicable)

1.2 Academic & Usage Information

  • Enrolled courses and subscription details
  • Lesson completion status
  • Quiz attempts, scores, and performance history
  • Task submissions (including uploaded images)
  • Attendance records for offline classes
  • Course completion forecasts and learning preferences

1.3 Payment Information

  • Transaction reference IDs
  • Payment status and history

Note: We do not store card or banking details. Payments are processed securely via authorized payment gateways.

1.4 Device & Technical Data

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and basic log information
  • Notification preferences

1.5 Camera Permission Usage

Our app requests camera access for the following educational purposes:

  • Document Capture: Students can capture and upload study materials, assignments, worksheets, and homework documents directly through the app
  • Task Submissions: Camera is used to photograph and submit completed tasks and assignments to instructors
  • Study Material Digitization: Students can photograph physical notes, textbooks, or reference materials for personal study use

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide access to subscribed courses and content
  • Track learning progress and performance
  • Conduct quizzes, tasks, and assessments
  • Manage offline attendance and notifications
  • Send alerts, reminders, and announcements
  • Generate reports for academic and administrative purposes
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience

3. Notifications & Communication

Users may receive notifications via:

  • In-app notifications
  • Push notifications
  • Email
  • WhatsApp (where enabled and consented)

Parents may also receive attendance and progress updates.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All user data is securely stored using industry-standard security practices.
  • Sensitive data is encrypted where required.
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized admin and staff only.
  • We regularly monitor and update security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent user data.

Data may be shared only:

  • With authorized staff and administrators for academic purposes
  • With payment gateways for transaction processing
  • When required by law or government authorities

6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access and review their personal data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Update learning preferences (as permitted)
  • Request account deactivation or data deletion (subject to institutional policies)

7. Attendance & Parental Access

  • Attendance data for offline students is recorded by staff through the admin panel.
  • Students and parents can view attendance reports anytime.
  • Attendance notifications may be sent through app alerts and WhatsApp.

8. Third-Party Services

The platform may integrate with:

  • Payment gateways
  • Notification services (email, SMS, WhatsApp)
  • Video platforms (e.g., YouTube)
  • Camera Permission: Used for capturing study documents and task submissions (images are stored securely and used only for educational purposes)

These third parties follow their own privacy policies.
